a word about us

Since our beginnings Foodhub's passion has been to offer fresh food by using quality ingredients. Originally based out of Canada, our strong customer base has allowed us to open in various parts of country like India, United States of America. All preparations are made from scratch daily and dishes are cooked to order. We use the freshest and highest quality ingredients. To our fellow and honorary Canadians, we hope you are reminded of home each time you eat at Foodhub. As for our first time visitors, we welcome you and promise to expand your palate with some of our favorite Canadian foods.
